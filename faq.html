Frequently Asked Questions

Where to buy ittromaa products?

You can purchase our handbags on Amazon USA and Amazon Canada.

What is the material and quality of ittromaa bags?

Our bags are made from the highest quality microfiber material, lined with soft cotton fabric, and equipped with top-quality hardware. We ensure that every bag is durable and long-lasting.

What is the return policy?

All items can be returned in accordance with Amazon’s return policy.

How are ittromaa bags better than others?

Our bags are designed in-house in Canada with great attention to detail, a focus on quality, and without using any animal products.

Why choose vegan handbags?

Choosing vegan handbags supports animal welfare, reduces environmental impact, and offers stylish, cruelty-free alternatives without compromising on quality or elegance.

Are the bags waterproof?

Our microfiber material offers a high degree of water resistance, making the bags suitable for everyday use in various weather conditions.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship within the USA and Canada via Amazon. For other locations, please check Amazon’s international shipping options.

How to care for my ittromaa handbag?

We recommend wiping the bag with a damp cloth and avoiding harsh chemicals to maintain its beauty and longevity.

Are ittromaa bags ethically made?

Yes, we carefully select manufacturers who share our values for ethical and sustainable production.

Can I track my order?

Yes, orders placed through Amazon come with tracking information for your convenience.
