Frequently Asked Questions

Where to buy ittromaa products?

You can purchase ittromaa bags conveniently on Amazon USA and Amazon Canada.

What is the material and quality of ittromaa bags?

Our bags are crafted from the highest quality microfiber material with a soft cotton lining and premium hardware. Designed for durability, ittromaa bags are long-lasting while maintaining an elegant look.

What is the return policy?

All ittromaa products are eligible for return according to Amazon’s return policy. Please refer to Amazon’s official return guidelines for detailed instructions.

How are ittromaa bags better than others?

Every ittromaa bag is designed in-house in Canada with meticulous attention to detail. We focus on quality materials and craftsmanship, and all our products are 100% vegan, with no use of animal-derived materials.

Why choose vegan?

Choosing vegan products means supporting cruelty-free fashion that does not rely on animal materials. Vegan bags from ittromaa provide a sustainable, ethical alternative without compromising on style, quality, or durability—helping protect animals and reduce environmental impact.

Are ittromaa bags waterproof or water-resistant?

Our microfiber material offers a degree of water resistance, helping protect your bag from light rain and spills. However, we recommend avoiding heavy exposure to water to maintain the bag’s quality.

How do I care for and clean my ittromaa bag?

To keep your bag looking new, wipe it gently with a damp cloth and mild soap when needed. Avoid harsh chemicals or soaking the bag. For stubborn stains, spot clean and allow it to air dry naturally.

Do you offer warranties on ittromaa products?

We stand behind the quality of our bags. Please check the product details or contact us directly for information about warranty coverage and support.

Can I find ittromaa bags in physical retail stores?

Currently, ittromaa bags are primarily sold online through Amazon USA and Amazon Canada. We’re exploring retail partnerships and will update customers when new locations become available.

Are ittromaa bags suitable for everyday use?

Absolutely! Our bags are designed for durability and style, perfect for everyday wear whether you’re heading to work, errands, or social outings.
